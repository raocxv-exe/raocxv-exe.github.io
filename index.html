Design process

In my UX/UI design process, I incorporate empathy to understand users' needs and emotions, creating solutions that resonate with them. Aesthetics play a crucial role in crafting visually appealing and engaging interfaces. User research helps me gather valuable insights to make informed decisions and design user-centered experiences.

  • 1. General needs
  • 2. Identifying the problem
  • 3. Putting myself in their shoes
  • 4. Solutions and more solutions
  • 5. Prototyping and testing

1. Is essential to me because it ensures that I can create meaningful and impactful experiences. By understanding user needs, I can design solutions that address their pain points, enhance usability, and ultimately deliver products that resonate with and delight users.
2. User research, gather feedback, and analyze user behaviors. By closely examining user interactions, jobs they must do, gains and pains, I can uncover identify the problem and extreme user that also have that problem.
3. I immerse myself in their context, pain points, and goals, allowing me to understand their perspective. This empathetic approach guides me in designing intuitive and user-centric interfaces that address their specific needs.
4. Creating dozens of solutions, no matter how crazy the solutions may sound, but of course respecting the requirements I select the ones that makes the most sense and fun to do.
5. I make prototypes of said solutions and test them with the users, the extreme user and persons that don’t have the same problems, if one is a clear winner then I take the feedback and fix or change what needs it.
