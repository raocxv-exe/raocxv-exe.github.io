This one is my personal favorite. The app is made from scrath to "no one", we had to solve one problem and I came up with this. I had the idea of a app like a tamagotchi, the design went through a lot of iterations I settle in this one at the third sprint, it’s some mixture between material design and personal touches. Was a blast working on this one, using gamification (as a gamer myself) in the UX made this one a lot of fun, but also quite challenging because I didn’t want it to be too much like a game, it’s still in development I have a few changes on the list.

● Developed an android application using Flutter that allows users have a pet and keep a record of their meals and feelings.
● Supported by a psychologist and with the use of Journaling.
● Design with gamification in mind to improve user experience and to keep it trigger free.
