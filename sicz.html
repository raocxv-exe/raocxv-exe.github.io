Some constraint in the design were that because this is a government department, the base design and color needed to be like other of their web pages, that’s why the design is extremely minimalist because our user are people over 40 years old and may have not so much experience with computers, also the computers in which this system is going to be used are not the best ones in terms of power so no animation or any other crazy element. Turn out nice, the clients were extremely pleased.

● Solved the problem of the lack of access to the cultural information of my state by developing a system for the Zacatecas Cultural Institute (IZC) focused on the preservation, updating, and modernization of cultural information and data storage for the state of Zacatecas, Mexico.
